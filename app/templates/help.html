Step-by-Step Instructions

Uploading Images
  1. Click the "Upload" button or go to the Upload page
  2. Drag and drop your image file or click "Browse Files"
  3. Wait for the preview to appear
  4. Click "Upload Image" to save it to your account
Transforming Images
  1. Go to your Dashboard and click on an image
  2. Click the "Transform" button (magic wand icon)
  3. Adjust the settings in the transformation panels
  4. Click "Apply Transformations" to process
